Section 03 — The Headline Insight

Two identical homes on your street. One leased in 33 days. One took 96.

The Most Important Data Point in This Report
3869 Plainview and 3912 Plainview are the same property type. Same street. Same builder. Same 1,881 sqft. Same 2019 build year.

3869 Plainview was priced at $2,400 — it took 96 days to lease. The owner held the price firm, the property leased at full ask, but they paid for it with 96 days of vacancy. At a $2,400/mo rent, that is roughly $7,680 in lost rent just sitting empty.

3912 Plainview was priced at $2,200 — it leased in 33 days. Same property type, same street, two months earlier off the market and producing income.

The $200/mo difference cost the 3869 owner approximately $4,620 in net income compared to the 3912 owner. Pricing right beats pricing high.
